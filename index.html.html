2. Permissions We Request

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

2.1 Camera/Flashlight

Purpose: To control the device's LED flashlight.

Note: We do not take photos or record video. This permission is only used to toggle the flashlight on and off.

2.2 Phone State (Optional)

Purpose: To enable flash alerts for incoming calls (if you enable this feature).

Note: We do not access your call logs, contacts, or phone numbers.

2.3 SMS (Optional)

Purpose: To enable flash alerts for incoming text messages (if you enable this feature).

Note: We do not read, store, or transmit your SMS content.

2.4 Vibrate

Purpose: To provide haptic feedback when flash alerts are triggered.

2.5 Foreground Service

Purpose: To run flash alert notifications in the background when enabled.

2.6 Internet Access

Purpose: To display advertisements and check for in-app purchases.
